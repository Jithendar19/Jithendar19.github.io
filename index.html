About Me

"If you don't go for your dreams, what is your life about?"

Jithendar is currently a Graduate Research Assistant at Texas A&M University - Commerce. Jithendar is part of the NSF team in the department of Computer Science where he is developing smart algorithms for analyzing RNA secondary structures. Jithendar interned with Electric Power Research Institute last summer where he developed a simulation software for a solar smart inverter. He is also two time winner of Programming Challenge conducted by the department of Computer Science annually. Before coming to masters, he was associated with Wipro Technologies as an Associate Software Engineer. In his free time, he plays badmiton and loves watching movies. He is excited by the technology and is focused on building softwares that will have a great impact in the society.
